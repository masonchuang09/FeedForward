Our Mission

Good food shouldn't go to waste while people go hungry. We recover what would be thrown away and route it to the organizations feeding our community.

What We Do

Three steps, the same day.

A simple, repeatable process keeps food fresh and gets it where it's needed fast.

01

Recover

We collect fresh, unserved surplus from school kitchens and local partners before it's discarded.

02

Package

Volunteers sort, portion, and safely pack each recovery to food-safety standards, ready for transport.

03

Deliver

We hand off to trusted partners — shelters, pantries, and youth programs — who get it onto plates.
