About

Good food, from kitchens to the people who need it.

We're a student-run food recovery initiative based at Cary Academy, turning everyday surplus into reliable meals for families across the Triangle.

Our Story

It started with a tray of untouched food.

FeedForward began in the Cary Academy cafeteria, where our founders kept noticing the same thing: trays of fresh, perfectly good food being thrown out at the end of every service — while families just minutes away went without.

It didn't add up. So we built a simple, repeatable system to recover that surplus, package it safely, and route it to the partners already doing the work of feeding our community.

What began as one cafeteria is now a growing network of kitchens, restaurants, and nonprofits across the Triangle — all moving good food forward instead of into the trash.
